Favorite Food & Drink

Explore some of my all-time favorite food and drink items.

Asam Pedas Ayam

Asam Pedas Ayam has been my favorite food since I was a kid. It is renowned for its strong flavours, combining the acidity of tamarind with the fiery heat of the chilli.

Ayam Masak Kicap

Ayam Masak Kicap is a popular Malaysian dish, known for its rich, savory, and somewhat sweet flavour, typically made with soy sauce.

Samyang Hot Chicken Carbonara

Samyang Hot Chicken Carbonara is a spicy noodle dish, with a creamy sauce mixed with spicy chicken.

Milk Tea

Milk tea, a popular beverage worldwide, combines tea with milk or other substitutes, creating a creamy, soothing drink.

Orange Juice

Orange juice is a refreshing, tangy beverage enjoyed globally, made by juicing fresh oranges.

Sirap Bandung

Sirap Bandung is a sweet, refreshing beverage made with rose syrup and condensed milk, popular in Malaysia and Singapore.
